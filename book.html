About The Book

Downloading Grace from Heaven will give you the missing piece in your Christian life – God’s grace! The entire spiritual dimension of life is free. It is received not earned. It is given and not paid for! Spiritual things such as love, joy and peace come to us by believing and receiving. DGFH will show you how this works. You will learn how to have instant self-control, instant joy and how to obtain wisdom from God. Just by believing and receiving the grace of God into your inner being through faith. This book will make the Bible come alive to you as the promises of Jesus Christ become your keys to Christian living.

Chapters of the Book

  1. The Mysterious Grace of God

    What grace is, how it operates, how it is unusual.

  2. The Kingdom of Heaven Is at Hand

    God’s blessings are available to every believing Christian through repentance and faith.

  3. From Spiritual Defeat to Spiritual Victory

    How our attitude to the Holy Spirit, who is the Spirit of Grace, is the key difference between living a defeated and toxic Christian life, and a gracious and victorious Christian life.

  4. The Grace of the Lord Jesus Christ be With Your Spirit

    How the Spirit of God works in the spirit of believers, to transform them into mature saints in the image of God.

  5. Grace and Works

    Looks at the tension between God’s grace and our good works and how we are saved by grace alone, through faith alone, in order to bear spiritual fruit and do good works.

  6. Praying for Grace

    How to pray for more grace in your life, and how to obtain grace for specific problem issues.

  7. Believing That You Will Receive Grace from Heaven

    How faith is vital to the work of grace in your Christian life.

  8. Receiving and Experiencing Grace from Heaven

    How having the right spiritually receptive attitudes will lead us to being blessed by God.

  9. Continuing in Heavenly Grace

    How to stay spiritually on-track in the life of grace by: avoiding temptation, abiding in Christ, dealing with stress and hurry, and avoiding emotional hijacking in times of perceived threat.

  10. The Stewardship of Grace

    How to be good stewards of the grace of God by exercising our spiritual gifts in love and in the fruit of the Holy Spirit.

  11. Walking in the Grace That You Have Received

    How to have a Spirit-led daily lifestyle that is based around receiving the grace of God.

  12. The Prayer of Jesus

    How the Lord’s Prayer summarizes the concept of downloading grace from Heaven.
